Algoholics

Do you love challenges?

Is getting things done systematically your thing?
Can you adapt to the challenges life throws at you?
If Yes, test your level of adaptation with Sankalan'20's Algoholics because like minds think alike!
Let's see how much dynamic can your programming be under the pressure of real-world challenges.

Are you Ready to Go?
This is the time to show your analytical and logic building capabilities. Algoholics offers you the opportunity to exceed the limit of problem-solving and show why your solution is 'optimum'.

INSTRUCTIONS

The event consists of two rounds:

1. Prelims

  1. This is an eliminating round.
  2. This round consists of 30 MCQs.
  3. Time limit: 30 minutes.

2. Mains

Shortlisted teams will be provided with a set of problems that they have to solve overnight. No real coding is required just an approach stating how you reached the solution that you propose.

TEAM SIZE

Single or Pair
