Cipher-O-More

To all the Jagga Jasoos out there! Sankalan'20 is back with The Cryptography event of DUCS

Do you think you have a hidden detective inside you who can crack any password or decode any hidden message?
If yes, then we welcome you to the interactive journey of this secret world where you find a lot of cryptic coded words that go to certain ways.
We expect you to come along with your Mr. Watson and wake up sherlock in you and solve some encoded and cryptic texts.

INSTRUCTIONS

ROUNDS

  • Preliminary round tests your cryptic knowledge as it contains question-related to cryptography and encoding/decoding. This will be an online round
  • For the finals, We expect you to turn into modern Julius Ceaser and find messages that are encoded in the text.
